About Me

I am a physics PhD candidate at Harvard University. I am working with Prof. Ashvin Vishwanath on strongly correlated physics. In Summer 2025, I will be at the Boulder Summer School. I will be applying to postdoc positions in Fall 2025. My interests lie in studying emergent phenomena in condensed matter systems analytically and numerically. In my spare time, I enjoy music, birding, hiking, and reading.
